A user owned internet

A user owned internet, where actors Can't Be Evil, is the general idea behind the Stacks network. By enabling inherent privacy and security, users are in control. The Ryder project on Stacks makes it easy to securly use your private keys. The Blockchain Naming System, a smart contract on Stacks gives public keys a human readable alternative and other important advantages to help achieve the general goal. A system for self souvereign identities (ssi) and decentralizes identities (DIDs) all secured through the Bitcoin network. This is just the beginning, users owning the internet is ultimate goal. That goals is what the Stacks community aims to build on the Stacks network; a level 1 blockchain anchored to bitcoin.
